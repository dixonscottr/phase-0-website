Sasayama: more than black beans

What makes Sasayama famous?

  • Black beans
  • Wild boar stew
  • The ruins of Sasayama Castle
  • The Dekansho summer festival
  • An uncomfortably slimy mountain yam
  • Tamba Tachikui-yaki pottery fired in "mountain-climbing kilns"
  • Kicking off the the "Great Heisei-Era Municipal Mergers" in 1999 by merging 4 towns into 1 city
